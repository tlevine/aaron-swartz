Racism in a lab coat: a longitudinal audit of discrimination by medical students [PDF]
An interesting study that finds that medical students become more racist as they get more medical school training. Racism is measured by hiring actors to perform identical symptoms. (The role of a lifetime!) The authors propose this is evidence for something about medical training that results in racist treatment.
