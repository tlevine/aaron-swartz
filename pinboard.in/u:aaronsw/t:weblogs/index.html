Ezra Klein's blog
I rarely recommend whole blogs, but Ezra's become an addictive must-read. He's really grown into it this year and it's getting to the point where I'm almost unhealthily obsessed with him. Ezra's kind of me as I'd like to imagine myself: more informed, more influential, wittier, and a better writer. His blog tackles the intellectual aspects of DC: policy wonkery, strategic analysis, and food. My only complaint is that I wish he covered other topics with the same depth he uses for health care.
