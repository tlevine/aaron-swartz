DFW: David Lynch Keeps His Head [in ASFTINDA]
By far, the most amazing article I have ever read. It's very long but never loses your interest; it combines an incredibly-detailed knowledge of cinematic history with a more thoughtful reflection and analysis than you'll see in any movie review with a classic DFW first-person description of the set. The whole thing is just so educational and thought-provoking and really uses its length to actually _work through_ issues instead of just glossing them that reading it feels like completing an entire college course.
